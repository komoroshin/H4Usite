The Challenge

A Growing Clinic Struggling with Paperwork

GavCure Clinic, a thriving private practice in Israel, faced a common but critical problem: their physicians were spending more time on documentation than actual patient care.

  • Unstructured intake process — Paper forms and manual questionnaires created inconsistent patient records
  • 15+ minutes per patient — Physicians spent the first quarter of each appointment gathering history
  • Patient drop-off after first visit — Long waits and repetitive questions frustrated patients
  • Limited capacity — The clinic couldn't grow without hiring more staff

The Solution

Implementing Healthy4U

GavCure deployed Healthy4U's AI platform to automate intake, documentation, and patient communication.

01

AI-Powered Intake

Patients complete structured questionnaires before their visit via the mobile app. The AI organizes responses into a comprehensive medical overview.

02

Ambient Documentation

AI-Scribe records and transcribes every consultation, automatically generating clinical notes that physicians can review and approve.

03

Unified Patient Records

All patient data — history, vitals, previous visits, lab results — consolidated into one continuously-updated health record.

04

Continuous Monitoring

Between visits, the platform tracks patient symptoms and vitals, alerting physicians to changes that require attention.

30% More Patients
0 New Hires
97% Less Admin Time

The Impact

Transformed Operations, Better Care

Within weeks of implementation, GavCure experienced a complete transformation in how they deliver care.

  • ✓ Standardized diagnostic flow ensuring consistent, high-quality care for every patient
  • ✓ Lower patient drop-off after first visits due to better experience
