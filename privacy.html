Privacy Policy

Healthy4U processes personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR where applicable.

Data We Process

  • account details,
  • health-related data uploaded by users,
  • usage and technical data.

Purpose of Processing

  • providing and securing the Service,
  • supporting Clinics and Patients,
  • legal and compliance obligations.

Data Sharing

Data may be shared with:

  • authorized Clinics and Clinicians,
  • infrastructure and hosting providers,
  • payment processors.

We do not sell personal data.

Data Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data by contacting privacy@healthy4u.world.

Data Processing Agreement (Summary)

Where a Clinic uploads Patient data, Healthy4U acts as a data processor, and the Clinic acts as data controller.

Healthy4U commits to:

  • process data only on documented instructions,
  • apply appropriate technical and organizational security measures,
  • assist with data subject rights requests,
  • notify of personal data breaches without undue delay.

A full DPA is available upon request.
